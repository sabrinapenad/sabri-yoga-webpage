ABOUT MY CLASSES
On my classes, all levels are welcomed. They are usually between 60 and 90 minutes.
As a teacher, my goal is for my students to create a connection with their bodies and their breathing, understanding their limitations and working around them. I trasmit all the benefits and the importance of performing yoga postures (asanas) without harming and without sacrificing your body, generating more strength, flexibility and resistance both mentally and physically.
I really like the spiritual side of yoga practice, I am passionate about teaching my students to live yoga inside and outside the mat. Usually, my classes begin with a small meditation in which I put a special intention to practice, followed by the practice of asanas (multilevel or basic, according to the experience of the group), and ending with a relaxation and final reflection.
